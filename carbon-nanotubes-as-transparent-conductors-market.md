<p><p>The transparent conductors market for carbon nanotubes (CNTs) includes Single-Walled Nanotubes (SWNTs), Double-Walled Nanotubes (DWNTs), and Multi-Walled Nanotubes (MWNTs). SWNTs, known for their exceptional electrical and optical properties, are ideal for applications requiring high transparency and conductivity. DWNTs combine features of both single and multi-walled structures, offering balanced performance. MWNTs, with multiple layers of carbon, provide robust conductivity but are generally less transparent. Each type serves specific needs in electronics, displays, and photovoltaic technologies, enhancing innovative applications.</p></p>
