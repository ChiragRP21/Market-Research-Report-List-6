<p><p>The automotive Bluetooth module market comprises three main types: Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) modules, Dual-mode Bluetooth modules, and Classic Bluetooth modules. BLE modules focus on energy-efficient communication, ideal for applications like vehicle tracking and monitoring. Dual-mode Bluetooth modules support both BLE and Classic Bluetooth, facilitating compatibility with a broader range of devices. Classic Bluetooth modules, offering robust data transfer capabilities, are commonly used for audio streaming and hands-free functions in vehicles, ensuring seamless connectivity and enhanced user experience.</p></p>
