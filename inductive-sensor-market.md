<p><p>Inductive sensor market can be categorized into two types: Fixed Distance and Adjustable Distance sensors. Fixed Distance sensors have a predetermined sensing range that cannot be changed, making them ideal for applications where consistent detection is required. On the other hand, Adjustable Distance sensors allow for the user to manually adjust the sensing range, providing flexibility in different applications. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various industries for detecting objects and controlling processes.</p></p>
