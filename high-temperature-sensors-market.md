<p><strong>The High Temperature Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resistance Temperature Detector</li><li>Thermocouple</li><li>Semiconductor-based Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-temperature sensors market comprises three primary types: Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTDs), thermocouples, and semiconductor-based sensors. RTDs utilize the change in electrical resistance with temperature, offering high accuracy and stability for industrial applications. Thermocouples, made from two dissimilar metals, generate a voltage in response to temperature differences, making them suitable for extreme temperatures. Semiconductor-based sensors leverage the thermal properties of semiconductors, providing compact size and rapid response times, ideal for electronic and automotive applications.</p></p>
