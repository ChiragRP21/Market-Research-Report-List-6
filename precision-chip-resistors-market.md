<p><strong>The Precision Chip Resistors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thin Film</li><li>Thick Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The precision chip resistors market primarily comprises two types: thin film and thick film resistors. Thin film resistors are made by depositing a uniform layer of resistive material on a ceramic substrate, offering high precision, low noise, and excellent performance in high-frequency applications. Thick film resistors, on the other hand, utilize a printed paste of resistive material, providing greater robustness and lower manufacturing costs, making them suitable for a wider range of applications. Both types serve different needs in electronic circuits.</p></p>
