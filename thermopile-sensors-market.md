<p><strong>The Thermopile Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermopile Infrared Sensors</li><li>Thermopile Laser Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermopile sensors market comprises two main types: thermopile infrared sensors and thermopile laser sensors. Thermopile infrared sensors detect temperature variations by measuring infrared radiation emitted from objects, commonly used in HVAC systems, medical devices, and consumer electronics. In contrast, thermopile laser sensors utilize focused laser beams to measure heat and radiation for precise applications, such as industrial automation and spectroscopy. Both types leverage thermoelectric principles to convert thermal energy into measurable electrical signals, enhancing performance across various industries.</p></p>
