<p><ul><li>Radiated and Conducted EMI Testing</li><li>Sensitivity and Immunity EMI Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EMI Precompliance Testers market encompasses two primary types: radiated and conducted EMI testing. Radiated testing measures emissions from devices affecting their environment, while conducted testing evaluates emissions through power and signal lines. Additionally, the market includes sensitivity and immunity testing, which assess a device's performance in the presence of electromagnetic interference. Sensitivity testing confirms how well a device can detect signals amid noise, while immunity testing determines its resilience against external EMI, ensuring reliable functionality in various environments.</p></p>
