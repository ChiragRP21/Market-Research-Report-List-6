<p><p>The gas phase filtration market includes three primary types: Activated Carbon Filters, Activated Alumina Filters, and Others. Activated Carbon Filters utilize porous carbon to adsorb organic compounds, odors, and volatile organic chemicals, making them ideal for air purification. Activated Alumina Filters, composed of aluminum oxide, effectively remove moisture and specific gases like fluoride and arsenic from air and water. The "Others" category encompasses various filtration technologies such as zeolites and ceramic filters, which serve specialized applications in gas treatment and environmental management.</p></p>
