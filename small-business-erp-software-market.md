<p><p>Small Business ERP Software is available in two main market types: On-Premises and Cloud-Based. On-Premises software is installed and maintained on the company's own servers, providing complete control over data and security. Cloud-Based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, offering flexibility and scalability. On-Premises software requires a higher upfront investment and ongoing maintenance, while Cloud-Based software typically has lower initial costs and regular subscription fees. Both options cater to different business needs and preferences.</p></p>
