<p><strong>The Chilled Beam Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Chilled Beams</li><li>Passive Chilled Beams</li><li>Multiservice Chilled Beams</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chilled beam systems are advanced HVAC solutions that use convection and radiation to cool spaces efficiently. Active chilled beams incorporate fan-based air circulation, allowing for precise temperature control and improved energy efficiency. Passive chilled beams rely solely on natural convection, making them quieter and less energy-intensive but less effective in controlling air movement. Multi-service chilled beams combine cooling, heating, and ventilation in a single unit, offering versatility in climate control while maximizing space utilization and energy savings.</p></p>
