<p><p>The elevator ropes market is categorized into three main types: Fiber Core (FC), Independent Wire Rope Core (IWRC), and Others. Independent Wire Rope Core ropes feature a core made of independent wires, providing enhanced strength and durability, ideal for heavy-duty applications.
