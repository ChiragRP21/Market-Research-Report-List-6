<p><p>The semi-permeable films market encompasses two main types: biomembranes and synthetic membranes. Biomembranes are natural materials derived from biological organisms, often used in medical and biotechnological applications due to their compatibility with living tissues. Synthetic membranes, on the other hand, are man-made fabrications designed for various industrial applications, including water purification and gas separation, offering tailored properties like chemical resistance and selective permeability. Both types play crucial roles in multiple sectors, enhancing efficiency and functionality in separation processes.</p></p>
