<p><p>The Single-Use Bioreactors (SUBs) market is categorized primarily into wave-induced motion SUBs and stirred SUBs, along with other emerging technologies. Wave-induced motion SUBs utilize gentle, oscillatory movements to promote mixing and oxygen transfer, minimizing shear stress on cells. In contrast, stirred SUBs employ mechanical agitation to enhance mixing and mass transfer. Other types may include disposable systems with innovative designs, such as airlift or internal loop reactors, catering to diverse bioprocessing needs and enhancing flexibility in production.</p></p>
