<p><p>An adsorption wheel, also known as a rotary adsorber or molecular sieve wheel, is a technology used primarily for air desiccation and volatile organic compound (VOC) recovery. It operates by rotating a series of adsorbent material beds that capture contaminants from the air stream, subsequently regenerating to release the pollutants in a concentrated form. This system is widely utilized in various applications, including HVAC systems, industrial processes, and environmental remediation.</p><p>The Adsorption Wheel Market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 4.2% during the forecast period.
