<p><p>The flu diagnosis market comprises three main types: Traditional Diagnostic Tests, Molecular Diagnostic Tests, and Other Molecular Tests. Traditional tests, like rapid influenza diagnostic tests, provide quick results based on antigen detection but may lack sensitivity. Molecular Diagnostic Tests, such as PCR assays, offer high accuracy by detecting viral RNA, facilitating early diagnosis. Other Molecular Tests include isothermal amplification methods and next-generation sequencing, which enhance detection capabilities and can identify multiple strains simultaneously, improving overall flu management and surveillance.</p></p>
