<p><p>A fixed-gear bicycle, often referred to as a "fixie," is a type of bike that has a single gear with the rear wheel directly connected to the pedals. Fixed-gear bicycles are popular among urban cyclists for their simplicity, low maintenance, and lightweight design. </p><p>The Fixed-Gear Bicycle Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.7% during the forecast period.
