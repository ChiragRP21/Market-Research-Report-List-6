<p><p>The mechanical micropipette market is primarily segmented into single-channel and multi-channel types. Single-channel micropipettes are designed for precise liquid handling in individual sample containers, making them ideal for applications requiring high accuracy. In contrast, multi-channel micropipettes allow for simultaneous transfer of liquids across multiple wells or samples, enhancing efficiency in high-throughput experiments. Both types are essential in laboratories for tasks such as dispensing reagents, performing dilutions, and conducting assays, each serving specific research needs and workflows.</p></p>
