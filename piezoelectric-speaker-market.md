<p><p>Piezoelectric speakers can be divided into two main types: Active Piezo and Passive Piezo. </p><p>Active Piezo speakers require an external power source to produce sound. They are commonly used in applications where high sound output is required, such as alarms and loudspeakers.</p><p>Passive Piezo speakers do not require an external power source to produce sound. They are often used in applications where low power consumption is important, such as in personal electronics like watches and calculators. Both types of speakers utilize the piezoelectric effect to convert electrical signals into sound waves.</p></p>
