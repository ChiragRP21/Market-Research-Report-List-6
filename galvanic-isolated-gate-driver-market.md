<p><p>The Galvanic Isolated Gate Driver market includes two main types: Isolated IGBT Gate Drivers and Isolated MOSFET Gate Drivers. Isolated IGBT Gate Drivers are designed for driving insulated gate bipolar transistors, typically used in high-power applications, providing robust isolation and protection. Isolated MOSFET Gate Drivers are tailored for driving metal-oxide-semiconductor field-effect transistors, known for their efficiency in switching applications. Both types ensure electrical isolation, enhancing safety and performance in power electronics by preventing unwanted feedback and reducing noise.</p></p>
