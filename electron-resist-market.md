<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electron Resist Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Electron Resist</li><li>Negative Electron Resist</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electron resist market is categorized into positive and negative electron resists, used primarily in semiconductor manufacturing and lithography processes. Positive electron resists become soluble when exposed to electron beams, allowing for the selective removal of material and enabling detailed patterning. Conversely, negative electron resists become insoluble upon exposure, resulting in undesired areas being removed while the exposed regions remain. Both types are critical for producing intricate semiconductor devices, contributing to advancements in electronics and nanotechnology.</p></p>
