<p><p>Ethylene Propylene Diene Rubber (EPDM) is a versatile synthetic rubber known for its excellent resistance to weathering, heat, and chemicals. The EPDM market can be categorized into two main types based on the polymerization process: solution polymerization and suspension polymerization. Solution polymerization involves dissolving the monomers in a solvent, allowing for precise control over the molecular structure, which enhances rubber quality. In contrast, suspension polymerization uses water as a medium, resulting in larger particles and lower production costs, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
