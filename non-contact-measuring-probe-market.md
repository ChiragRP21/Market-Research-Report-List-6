<p><p>The non-contact measuring probe market primarily includes machine tool probes and coordinate measuring machine (CMM) probes. Machine tool probes facilitate precise measurement and inspection during machining processes, ensuring quality control and optimization of manufacturing operations. CMM probes, on the other hand, are used in specialized machines to assess the geometry of physical objects, enhancing accuracy in dimensional measurements. Both types rely on advanced technology, improving efficiency and reliability in industrial applications by minimizing direct contact with the workpiece.</p></p>
