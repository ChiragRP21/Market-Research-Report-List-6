<p><p>Personal GPS trackers are available in two main types: SIM GPS trackers and SIM free GPS trackers. SIM GPS trackers require a SIM card to communicate location data to a designated phone or server. On the other hand, SIM free GPS trackers do not require a SIM card and instead use other methods such as Bluetooth or Wi-Fi to transmit data. Both types offer different pros and cons based on factors like coverage, cost, and ease of use.</p></p>
