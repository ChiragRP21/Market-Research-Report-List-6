<p><ul><li>Synthesis Machining Fluid</li><li>Semi-Synthetic Machining Fluid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The machining fluid market consists of two primary types: synthesis machining fluids and semi-synthetic machining fluids. Synthesis machining fluids are fully formulated products derived from chemical compounds, offering excellent lubricating properties and thermal stability for various machining processes. In contrast, semi-synthetic machining fluids are a blend of synthetic and mineral oils, providing a balance of lubrication and cooling, while also being cost-effective. Both types are essential for improving efficiency, extending tool life, and enhancing surface finish in machining operations.</p></p>
