<p><p>The Terahertz Imaging System market is divided into two main types: Passive and Active Terahertz Imaging. Passive Terahertz Imaging relies on natural radiation emitted by objects, capturing their thermal signatures without any external energy input. This method is often used for security and surveillance applications. In contrast, Active Terahertz Imaging involves the use of external terahertz sources to illuminate subjects, enhancing imaging quality and depth. This technique is commonly applied in material characterization and biomedical research, providing detailed insights.</p></p>
