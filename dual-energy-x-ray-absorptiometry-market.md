<p><p>The Dual-energy X-ray Absorptiometry (DXA) market is segmented into axial and peripheral bone densitometers. Axial bone densitometers are primarily used for central measurement sites like the spine and hip, providing detailed assessments of bone mineral density and fracture risk. On the other hand, peripheral bone densitometers are designed for measuring non-central sites, such as the wrist and heel, offering portable and cost-effective options for assessing bone health, especially in outpatient settings or screening scenarios.</p></p>
