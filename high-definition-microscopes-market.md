<p><p>The High Definition Microscopes market is primarily divided into two types: Optical Microscopes and Electron Microscopes. Optical Microscopes utilize visible light and lenses to magnify specimens, making them ideal for biological and educational applications. They offer ease of use and a wide field of view. In contrast, Electron Microscopes employ electron beams for higher resolution imaging at the nanometer scale, enabling detailed observation of cellular structures and materials. Each type serves distinct research and industrial needs, driving advancements in various scientific fields.</p></p>
