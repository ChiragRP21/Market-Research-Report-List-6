<p><strong>The E Chuck for Wafer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>E Chuck technology in the wafer market encompasses two distinct types: Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type. Coulomb Type utilizes electrostatic forces for precise wafer gripping, enabling stable handling during processing. In contrast, JR Type combines mechanical clamping and electrostatic forces for enhanced stability and versatility. Both types serve critical roles in semiconductor manufacturing, providing efficient solutions for wafer positioning and minimizing defects, thus improving overall yield in the increasingly complex and miniaturized landscape of semiconductor fabrication.</p></p>
