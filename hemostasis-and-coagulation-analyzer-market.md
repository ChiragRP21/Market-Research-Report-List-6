<p><p>Hemostasis and coagulation analyzers play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring clotting disorders. The market includes routine coagulation tests like Prothrombin Time (PT) and International Normalized Ratio (INR) for assessing vitamin K-dependent clotting factors. Activated Partial Thromboplastin Time (APTT) evaluates intrinsic pathway factors, while Thrombin Time (TT) assesses fibrinogen functionality. Fibrinogen quantification and D-dimer tests aid in thrombosis evaluation. Additionally, diagnostic assays for platelet function and thrombophilia testing are essential for understanding bleeding risks and clotting abnormalities.</p></p>
