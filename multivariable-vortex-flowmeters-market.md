<p><p>Multivariable Vortex Flowmeters are available in two main types - Inline Type and Insertion Type. The Inline Type is directly installed in the pipe or process line, providing continuous measurement of flow without interrupting the flow path. On the other hand, the Insertion Type is inserted into the pipe or vessel through a tapping point, making it suitable for applications where inline installation is not possible. Both types offer accurate measurement of flow, making them essential in various industries such as oil & gas, chemical, and water treatment.</p></p>
