<p><p>The in-flight entertainment display market can be categorized into two main types: stationary and handheld. Stationary displays are fixed screens integrated into the aircraft seatbacks, providing a consistent viewing experience for passengers. They are typically larger and offer a variety of content options. Handheld displays, on the other hand, are portable devices, such as tablets or laptops, that passengers can use during the flight. These provide a personalized viewing experience, offering flexibility and convenience for travelers.</p></p>
