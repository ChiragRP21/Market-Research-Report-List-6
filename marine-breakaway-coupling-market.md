<p><p>The Marine Breakaway Coupling market features two primary types: Petal Valve and Flip-Flap couplings. Petal Valve Marine Breakaway Couplings utilize a petal-shaped valve that quickly closes upon disconnection, effectively preventing spills. Flip-Flap Marine Breakaway Couplings use a hinged flap mechanism to seal off the flow in case of a breakaway, minimizing the risk of leakage. Both types are crucial for ensuring safety in marine transfer operations, providing reliable protection against accidental disconnections while maintaining operational efficiency.</p></p>
