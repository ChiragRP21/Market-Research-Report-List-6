<p><p>The hair mask market encompasses two main types: steam-free hair masks and thermal steam hair masks. Steam-free hair masks are typically oil or cream-based treatments designed to nourish and hydrate hair without the need for heat, making them convenient for at-home use. In contrast, thermal steam hair masks utilize heat to open hair cuticles, allowing deeper penetration of ingredients for enhanced nourishment and repair. Both types cater to different hair care needs and preferences, appealing to diverse consumer demographics.</p></p>
