<p><ul><li>Dry-packed</li><li>Fluid Packaged</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Non-Shrink Cement Grout market comprises two primary types: dry-packed and fluid packaged. Dry-packed grout is a dense mixture used for filling voids and providing high compressive strength, suitable for applications like anchoring and underpinning. In contrast, fluid packaged grout is formulated for easy application and flow, ideal for filling gaps and ensuring complete bonding in construction projects. Both types are designed to minimize shrinkage, maintain structural integrity, and enhance durability in various construction settings.</p></p>
