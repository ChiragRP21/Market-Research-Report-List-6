<p><p>The button cell market comprises several types of batteries, each suited for specific applications. Lithium button cells (CR) offer high energy density and long shelf life, making them ideal for watches and medical devices. Silver oxide batteries (SR) provide stable voltage and are commonly used in precision devices like watches and hearing aids. Alkaline button cells (LR) are cost-effective and suitable for low-drain devices. Zinc-air batteries, primarily used in hearing aids, provide high energy output. Other types include hybrid and rechargeable variants.</p></p>
