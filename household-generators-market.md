<p><p>The household generators market is primarily divided into two types: portable and standby generators. Portable generators are compact, versatile, and designed for temporary power needs, making them ideal for outdoor activities or immediate backup during outages. They typically run on gasoline or propane. In contrast, standby generators are permanently installed and automatically activate during power failures, providing seamless, continuous electricity to ensure home safety and comfort. Standby units usually operate on natural gas or propane and are more powerful, catering to greater energy demands.</p></p>
