<p><p>A Surface Acoustic Wave Filter, also known as an SAW filter, is an electronic device that uses acoustic waves on the surface of a piezoelectric material to filter and process electrical signals. <p><p>Surface Acoustic Wave Filters are categorized into two market types based on their construction - Lead Wire and Without Lead Wire. Lead Wire filters are equipped with external lead wires for connection, while Without Lead Wire filters have their electrodes deposited directly on the substrate. Lead Wire filters are commonly used in applications requiring external connections, while Without Lead Wire filters are preferred for compact designs and applications where space is limited. Both types offer unique advantages and cater to different market needs.</p></p>
