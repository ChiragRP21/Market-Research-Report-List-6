<p><ul><li>Centrifugal</li><li>Electrostatic</li><li>Media</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mist collectors for machinetools come in three main types - centrifugal, electrostatic, and media. Centrifugal mist collectors work by spinning the mist-laden air to separate out the contaminants. Electrostatic mist collectors use an electric field to attract and capture the mist particles. Media mist collectors utilize filters or other porous materials to trap the mist particles as the air passes through. Each type of mist collector has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs and requirements of the machinetool operation.</p></p>
