<p><p>The non-metallic industrial gasket market features several types including spiral wound, ring joint, and corrugated gaskets. Spiral wound gaskets, composed of alternating metal and filler material, offer resilience and sealing efficiency under varying pressures and temperatures. Ring joint gaskets provide robust sealing for high-pressure applications through their metallic composition. Corrugated gaskets, made from layers of compressible material, ensure excellent sealing in irregular surfaces. Additional categories encompass specialty gaskets tailored for unique industrial needs, enhancing overall performance in various applications.</p></p>
