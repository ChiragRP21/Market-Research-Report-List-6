<p><ul><li>Polyester PU Films</li><li>Polyether PU Films</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PU films market comprises two primary types: polyester PU films and polyether PU films. Polyester PU films are characterized by their excellent abrasion resistance and strength, making them suitable for applications in automotive and industrial sectors. In contrast, polyether PU films offer superior flexibility and are more resistant to hydrolysis, making them ideal for medical and textile applications. Both types are valued for their durability and versatility, catering to diverse industry needs while enhancing product performance.</p></p>
