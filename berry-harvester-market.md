<p><p>The berry harvester market includes two main types: half-row and full-row harvesters. Half-row harvesters are designed to efficiently pick berries from one side of the row, suitable for narrower fields or specific crops. In contrast, full-row harvesters can harvest berries from both sides simultaneously, making them ideal for wider rows and maximizing productivity. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on the operation scale, berry variety, and farm layout, catering to different agricultural needs and improving harvesting efficiency.</p></p>
