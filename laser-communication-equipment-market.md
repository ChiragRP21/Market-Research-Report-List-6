<p><strong>The Laser Communication Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transmitter</li><li>Receiver</li><li>Modulator</li><li>Demodulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser communication equipment market comprises various segments, including transmitters, receivers, modulators, and demodulators. Transmitters convert electrical signals into laser beams for data transmission. Receivers detect incoming laser signals and convert them back into electrical signals. Modulators encode data onto the laser beam, facilitating efficient communication. Demodulators, on the other hand, extract the original information from the modulated laser signals. Together, these components enable high-speed and secure optical communication in various applications, including telecommunications and space technology.</p></p>
