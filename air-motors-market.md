<p><p>The air motors market comprises three primary types: vane air motors, piston air motors, and gear air motors. Vane air motors utilize rotating vanes to create torque, making them efficient for various applications. Piston air motors operate using reciprocating pistons, delivering high power and torque for heavy-duty tasks. Gear air motors employ a system of gears to create motion, offering torque control and compact design. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, contributing to the diverse landscape of air motor applications.</p></p>
