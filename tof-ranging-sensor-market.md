<p><strong>The ToF Ranging Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave ToF</li><li>Gate ToF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ToF Ranging Sensor Market Types include Continuous Wave ToF and Gate ToF. Continuous Wave ToF sensors emit a continuous signal of light and measure the time it takes for the light to reflect back, providing accurate distance measurement. On the other hand, Gate ToF sensors emit short pulses of light and measure the time it takes for the light to return, allowing for precise ranging within a specific range. Both technologies are widely used in applications such as robotics, industrial automation, and automotive safety systems.</p></p>
