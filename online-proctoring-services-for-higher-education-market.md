<p><p>Online proctoring services for higher education encompass three primary market types: live proctoring, automated proctoring, and recorded proctoring. Live proctoring involves a human proctor monitoring students in real-time via video feed, ensuring integrity during exams. Automated proctoring uses AI and software tools to detect suspicious behavior through algorithms, without human oversight. Recorded proctoring captures video and audio during the exam for later review, allowing institutions to verify compliance post-test. Each type offers distinct advantages in ensuring academic integrity.</p></p>
