<p><p>The embedded biometric sensor market comprises various types, each utilizing unique biological identifiers. Fingerprint identification captures and analyzes unique ridge patterns on fingers. Vein identification examines the unique patterns of veins beneath the skin. Iris identification focuses on the distinct patterns in the colored part of the eye. Voiceprint identification analyzes vocal characteristics and patterns for authentication. Other markets may include facial recognition and DNA scanning, broadening the scope of biometric technologies used for security and identification purposes.</p></p>
