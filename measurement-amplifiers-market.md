<p><ul><li>Differential Measurement Amplifier</li><li>Noise Measurement Amplifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The measurement amplifiers market comprises various types, including differential measurement amplifiers and noise measurement amplifiers. Differential measurement amplifiers are designed to amplify the difference between two input signals while rejecting common-mode noise, making them ideal for applications like sensor data acquisition. Noise measurement amplifiers, on the other hand, focus on optimizing the signal-to-noise ratio, providing enhanced accuracy in environments with significant background interference. Both types play critical roles in precise measurements across industrial, medical, and scientific applications.</p></p>
