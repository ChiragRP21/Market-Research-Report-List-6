<p><ul><li>Multi-Passage</li><li>Single-Passage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the rotary union market, there are two primary types: multi-passage and single-passage. Multi-passage rotary unions allow the simultaneous transfer of multiple fluids, such as air, water, and lubrication, through different channels, enhancing efficiency in complex machinery. In contrast, single-passage rotary unions focus on a single fluid pathway, typically used in simpler applications where only one fluid needs to be conveyed. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, influencing design choices based on fluid requirements and system complexity.</p></p>
