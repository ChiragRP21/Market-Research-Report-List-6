<p><ul><li>Parabolic Trough Systems</li><li>Power Tower Systems</li><li>Dish/Engine Systems</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Molten salt technology in the solar energy market primarily comprises Parabolic Trough Systems, Power Tower Systems, and Dish/Engine Systems. Parabolic Trough Systems utilize curved mirrors to concentrate sunlight onto a receiver, heating molten salt to generate steam for electricity. Power Tower Systems use a central receiver with multiple mirrors, achieving higher efficiency with storage capabilities. Dish/Engine Systems focus sunlight onto a Stirling engine, converting thermal energy into mechanical energy. Other systems may include innovative designs and hybrid technologies, enhancing overall efficiency and output.</p></p>
