<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode Optical Fiber</li><li>Multimode Optical Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical fiber market is divided into two main types: single mode and multimode optical fibers. Single mode fibers support a single light path, allowing for long-distance communication with minimal signal loss, making them ideal for telecommunications and data centers. Multimode fibers, on the other hand, support multiple light paths, facilitating shorter distance communications with higher bandwidth capabilities. Each type serves specific applications; single mode is preferred for long-range, while multimode is suited for shorter connections in commercial and industrial settings.</p></p>
