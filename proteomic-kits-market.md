<p><p>The proteomic kits market is divided into three main types: extraction kits, analysis kits, and sequencing kits. Extraction kits are designed to isolate proteins from biological samples, ensuring the integrity and purity necessary for subsequent analyses. Analysis kits focus on techniques such as mass spectrometry and chromatography to characterize and quantify proteins, while sequencing kits provide methods for determining the amino acid sequences of proteins. Together, these kits enable comprehensive protein study, driving advancements in research and diagnostics.</p></p>
