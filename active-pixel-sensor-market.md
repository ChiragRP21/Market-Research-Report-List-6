<p><strong>The Active pixel Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Buried Photodiode-structure</li><li>Selection Transistor</li><li>MOS Amplifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The active pixel sensor (APS) market is categorized into three main types: buried photodiode structure, selection transistor, and MOS amplifier. Buried photodiode structures enhance light sensitivity and reduce noise by placing photodiodes beneath the pixel layer. Selection transistors manage individual pixel readouts, enabling higher frame rates and improved image quality. MOS amplifiers provide signal amplification within each pixel, enhancing the dynamic range and overall performance. Together, these technologies compete in various applications, including consumer electronics and industrial imaging.</p></p>
