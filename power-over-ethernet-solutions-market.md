<p><strong>The Power Over Ethernet Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Power Sourcing Equipment Controllers & ICs</li><li>Powered Device Controllers & ICs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Power Over Ethernet (PoE) solutions market features two key segments: Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) controllers and integrated circuits (ICs) and Powered Device (PD) controllers and ICs. PSE controllers manage power delivery from devices like switches and injectors to powered devices, ensuring efficient energy transfer. Conversely, PD controllers and ICs regulate how powered devices, such as IP cameras and VoIP phones, receive and utilize the supplied power. Together, these components enable seamless, reliable power distribution over standard Ethernet cabling.</p></p>
