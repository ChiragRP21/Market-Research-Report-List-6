<p><strong>The LEO Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LEO antenna market is divided into two main types: active and passive. Active LEO antennas incorporate amplifying elements that enhance signal reception and transmission, enabling more robust communication with low Earth orbit satellites. They are often used in high-demand applications requiring reliable connectivity. Conversely, passive LEO antennas rely on the natural reception of signals, lacking internal amplification. While they can be simpler and cost-effective, passive antennas may not perform as effectively in challenging environments or for high-bandwidth needs.</p></p>
