<p><strong>The Linear Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Linear Encoders</li><li>Absolute Linear Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The linear encoders market comprises two primary types: incremental and absolute linear encoders. Incremental linear encoders provide position information based on changes in displacement, offering high speed and resolution, typically used in applications like robotic arms and factory automation. Conversely, absolute linear encoders maintain a unique position value throughout their range, allowing for immediate location recognition after power loss, making them ideal for applications requiring precise re-calibration, such as CNC machines and aerospace systems.</p></p>
