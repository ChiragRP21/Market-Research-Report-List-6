<p><p>The Automotive V2X Antenna Market encompasses two primary types: Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V) and Vehicle-to-Infrastructure (V2I). V2V communication enables vehicles to exchange information directly, enhancing safety by providing real-time data on traffic conditions and potential hazards. Conversely, V2I involves vehicles communicating with infrastructure elements, such as traffic lights and road signs, to optimize traffic flow and improve overall road safety. Both technologies are integral to the development of intelligent transportation systems and autonomous driving capabilities.</p></p>
