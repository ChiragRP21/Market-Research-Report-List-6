<p><p>The smoke extractor exhaust fan market includes several types, primarily categorized into centrifugal and axial fans, along with other variants. Centrifugal fans utilize a rotating impeller to draw smoke and gases away from the source, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. Axial fans, on the other hand, move air parallel to the fan's axis, suitable for lower-pressure environments with larger air volumes. Other types may include mixed-flow or specialized designs tailored for specific industrial or commercial needs, enhancing safety and air quality.</p></p>
