<p><p>The temperature measurement robot market includes two primary types: infrared imaging and thermal imaging. Infrared imaging utilizes sensors to detect heat emitted from objects, converting this data into visual images that identify temperature differences. Thermal imaging, on the other hand, captures and displays thermal radiation, providing precise temperature readings across surfaces. Both technologies enhance temperature monitoring in various applications, such as industrial processes, healthcare, and safety inspections, enabling rapid and non-contact measurement for improved efficiency and safety.</p></p>
