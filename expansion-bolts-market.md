<p><p>The expansion bolts market includes several types tailored for different applications. Stud anchors consist of a threaded rod fixed into concrete, providing strong load-bearing capacity. Sleeved anchors feature a cylindrical sleeve that expands within a drilled hole, ensuring secure fastening. Wedge anchors use a tapered end that wedges into the concrete as the bolt is tightened, creating a robust grip. Other market types may include toggle bolts and drop-in anchors, catering to various fastening needs in construction and industrial settings.</p></p>
