<p><ul><li>Single-Stage Torque Converter</li><li>Multistage Torque Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive torque converter market is primarily categorized into two types: single-stage and multistage torque converters. A single-stage torque converter utilizes a single impeller and turbine setup to facilitate power transmission, offering simplicity and efficiency for standard applications. In contrast, multistage torque converters incorporate multiple impellers and turbines, enabling enhanced performance and greater torque multiplication, which is beneficial for high-performance and heavy-duty vehicles. These distinct types cater to varying performance needs and vehicle applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
