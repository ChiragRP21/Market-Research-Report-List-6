<p><p>Powered surgical stapling devices come in three main types: straight, curved, and circular. Straight staplers are used for linear cuts and closure in surgeries, while curved staplers are designed for procedures in hard-to-reach areas or tight spaces. Circular staplers are specifically used for creating circular anastomoses in surgeries. Each type of stapler serves a specific purpose in the surgical setting, providing surgeons with options to efficiently and effectively close incisions and wounds.</p></p>
