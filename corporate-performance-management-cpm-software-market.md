<p><p>Corporate Performance Management (CPM) software is available in two main market types: On-Premises and Cloud-Based. Cloud-Based CPM software, on the other hand, is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, offering lower upfront costs, easier scalability, and improved accessibility but potentially raising concerns about data security and reliance on internet connectivity.</p></p>
