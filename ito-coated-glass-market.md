<p><ul><li>Un-polished Soda Lime</li><li>Polished OLED Soda Lime</li><li>Boro-silicate Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ITO coated glass is categorized into three main types: un-polished soda lime, polished OLED soda lime, and borosilicate glass. Un-polished soda lime is cost-effective and used in general applications where optical clarity isn't critical. Polished OLED soda lime features a smooth surface, enhancing light transmission for OLED displays, improving performance in high-end electronics. Boro-silicate glass offers superior thermal resistance and durability, making it suitable for demanding applications like laboratory equipment and high-performance devices. Together, these types fulfill diverse market needs.</p></p>
