<p><strong>The Intraoral Digital Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD Sensor</li><li>CMOS Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intraoral digital cameras market primarily features two sensor types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD sensors offer superior image quality and low noise, making them ideal for high-resolution dental imaging. However, they are generally more expensive. Conversely, CMOS sensors are more cost-effective, consume less power, and provide fast image capture, catering well to the increasing demand for affordable dental solutions. Both technologies contribute uniquely to enhancing dental diagnostics and patient care.</p></p>
