<p><p>The Virgin Containerboard Market comprises two primary types: linerboard and corrugating medium. Linerboard serves as the outer layer of corrugated boxes, providing strength and protection, while featuring a smooth surface for printing. Corrugating medium is the fluted layer sandwiched in between, offering cushioning and rigidity. Both types are made from fresh fibers, ensuring high strength and durability, making them essential for packaging industries that prioritize quality and sustainability in their products. Together, they enhance the performance of corrugated packaging.</p></p>
