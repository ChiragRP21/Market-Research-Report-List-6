<p><p>Rubber injection molding machinery comes in two main types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical machines have a compact design, allowing for efficient use of space and easy access for operators. They are ideal for small, intricate parts and often facilitate multi-shot molding. In contrast, horizontal machines are typically larger and allow for greater production volume. They are suited for bigger components and provide better visibility of the molding process. Both types cater to different production needs within the rubber manufacturing industry.</p></p>
