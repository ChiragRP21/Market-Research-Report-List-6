<p><p>The fuel spill containment market is categorized into two primary types: Remote-Fill Type and Direct-Fill Type. Remote-Fill Type systems allow for fuel to be transferred from a distance, minimizing exposure to hazards and enhancing safety during refueling operations. In contrast, Direct-Fill Type systems involve direct transfer of fuel into containers, offering simplicity and immediate access but potentially increasing spill risks. Both types are designed to prevent environmental contamination and ensure compliance with regulations, catering to different operational needs.</p></p>
