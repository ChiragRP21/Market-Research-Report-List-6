Rheolytic devices use high-pressure saline to remove plaque, while Aspiration devices use suction to extract thrombus. Rotational devices have a rotating tip to shred and remove obstructions, and Ultrasound devices use sound waves to break down clots. Each type of device offers unique benefits in treating arterial blockages and improving blood flow in patients with cardiovascular disease.</p></p>
