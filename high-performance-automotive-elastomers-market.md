<p><strong>The High Performance Automotive Elastomers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPE)</li><li>Thermosetting Elastomer (TSE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Performance Automotive Elastomers market is segmented into two primary types: Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPE) and Thermosetting Elastomers (TSE). TPEs are versatile materials that combine the elasticity of rubber with the recyclability of plastics, allowing for easy processing and shaping. They are commonly used in automotive seals, gaskets, and interior applications. TSEs, on the other hand, are cross-linked materials that provide superior heat, chemical resistance, and durability, making them ideal for high-stress components like engine mounts and wiring insulation.</p></p>
