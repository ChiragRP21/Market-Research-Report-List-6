<p><ul><li>Simplex Metal Lead Frame</li><li>Resin Molding Lead Frame</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED leadframe market encompasses various types of leadframes used in LED packaging, primarily including simplex metal leadframes and resin molding leadframes. Simplex metal leadframes are made from conductive materials, providing strong electrical connections and efficient heat dissipation, ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, resin molding leadframes integrate resin for improved insulation and protection against environmental factors, making them suitable for compact designs and lower-cost production. Both types cater to different lighting applications, balancing performance and manufacturing efficiency.</p></p>
