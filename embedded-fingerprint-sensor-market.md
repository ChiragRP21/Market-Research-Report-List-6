<p><p>The embedded fingerprint sensor market can be segmented based on interface types, primarily including UART and USB interfaces. The UART interface offers a serial communication method for connecting fingerprint sensors to microcontrollers, favored for its simplicity and low power consumption, making it ideal for compact devices. In contrast, the USB interface provides a faster data transfer rate and plug-and-play convenience, commonly used in larger applications like laptops and access control systems. Both interfaces cater to diverse technological needs and applications.</p></p>
