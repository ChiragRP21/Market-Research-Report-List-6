<p><p>Ammonia gas sensors market types include metal semiconductor, conductive polymer, electrochemistry, nanomaterials, optical fiber, and others. Metal semiconductor sensors rely on the change in electrical conductivity when exposed to ammonia gas. Conductive polymer sensors detect gases through changes in resistance. Electrochemistry sensors use chemical reactions to detect ammonia gas. Nanomaterial sensors utilize nanostructures to detect gas molecules. Optical fiber sensors detect gas concentration through light absorption. Other types of sensors may include photoionization detectors or thermal conductivity sensors.</p></p>
