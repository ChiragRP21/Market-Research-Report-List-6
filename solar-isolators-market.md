<p><p>The solar isolators market primarily consists of 2-pole and 4-pole configurations, each serving distinct applications in solar power systems. 2-pole isolators are typically used in single-phase installations, providing effective disconnection for maintenance and safety. In contrast, 4-pole isolators are designed for three-phase systems, allowing for disconnection of all phases and neutral, enhancing system reliability. Both types are essential for ensuring compliance with electrical standards and protecting solar installations from faults or maintenance disruptions.</p></p>
