<p><strong>The Autotransfusion Systems (ATS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Autotransfusion Systems</li><li>Discontinuous Autotransfusion Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Autotransfusion systems (ATS) are medical devices used to collect and reinfuse a patient's own blood during surgery, minimizing the need for donor blood. The market is segmented into continuous autotransfusion systems, which provide a steady flow of blood collection and reinfusion during procedures, ensuring real-time availability of the patient's blood. Discontinuous autotransfusion systems, on the other hand, collect blood at specific intervals, allowing for storage and subsequent reinfusion. Both types enhance patient safety and reduce blood transfusion-related complications.</p></p>
