<p><strong>The Graphic LCD Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Synchronous Controller</li><li>Asynchronous Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Graphic LCD controllers can be classified into two main types: Synchronous and Asynchronous controllers. Synchronous controllers are designed to operate in tandem with the display's refresh rate, ensuring smooth and synchronized updating of the screen. On the other hand, Asynchronous controllers function independently of the display's refresh rate, allowing for more flexibility and control over the visual output. Both types cater to different needs and applications in the market, offering varying levels of performance and functionality.</p></p>
