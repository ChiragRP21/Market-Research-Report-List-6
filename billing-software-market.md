<p><p>Billing software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based billing software operates via the internet, allowing users to access their systems remotely, ensuring automatic updates and scalability. This model often features subscription pricing, making it cost-effective for businesses of all sizes. In contrast, on-premises billing software is installed directly on a company’s servers, providing complete control over data and security. However, it requires significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance, making it suitable for organizations with specific IT infrastructure needs.</p></p>
