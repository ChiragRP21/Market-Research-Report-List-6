<p><p>The Oil & Gas Risers and Flowlines market consists of two main types: risers and flowlines. Risers are vertical pipes that transport hydrocarbons from the seabed to a floating facility, facilitating deepwater exploration. Flowlines, on the other hand, are horizontal pipelines connecting subsea wells to processing facilities onshore or offshore. Both systems are crucial for efficient and safe transportation of oil and gas, ensuring optimized production and minimizing environmental risks in offshore fields.</p></p>
