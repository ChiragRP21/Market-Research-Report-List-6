<p><p>The VOC Portable Gas Detector market is primarily segmented into two types: Photoionization Detectors (PIDs) and Gas Sensitive Semiconductors. PIDs utilize ultraviolet light to ionize gas molecules, enabling the detection of volatile organic compounds at low concentrations, making them ideal for environmental monitoring and industrial applications. In contrast, Gas Sensitive Semiconductor detectors rely on changes in electrical conductivity in response to gas exposure, offering a cost-effective solution for detecting specific gases. Both technologies cater to safety and compliance needs in various sectors.</p></p>
