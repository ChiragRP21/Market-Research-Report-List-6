<p><p>The pharmaceuticals excipients market encompasses various types, including binders, glidants, diluents, and disintegrants, each playing a crucial role in drug formulation. Binders help hold the tablet ingredients together, while glidants enhance the flow properties of powders. Diluents are used to bulk up formulations, ensuring the proper dosage, and disintegrants facilitate the breakdown of tablets in the digestive tract for effective drug release. Together, these excipients improve the stability, efficacy, and patient compliance of pharmaceutical products.</p></p>
