<p><p>Pet dog accident insurance offers two main types of coverage: lifetime cover and non-lifetime cover. Lifetime cover provides ongoing coverage for accidents and illnesses throughout the dog's life, ensuring that any conditions that arise are treated without a cap on payouts. In contrast, non-lifetime cover offers a more limited approach, where coverage for specific conditions ends once a maximum payout is reached or the policy is renewed. These options cater to different budget and care preferences among pet owners.</p></p>
