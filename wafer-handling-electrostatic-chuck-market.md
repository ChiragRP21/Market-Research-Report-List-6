<p><strong>The Wafer Handling Electrostatic Chuck Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chuck</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chuck</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wafer Handling Electrostatic Chuck market includes two primary types: Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks. Coulomb Type utilizes electrostatic forces to hold wafers, offering high stability and precision, making them ideal for semiconductor applications. JR Type, on the other hand, employs a dielectric layer to enhance adhesion and control, providing improved performance in various processes. Both types are crucial for maintaining wafer integrity during handling and processing in the semiconductor manufacturing industry.</p></p>
