<p><ul><li>Single Break Isolator</li><li>Double Break Isolator</li><li>Pantograph type Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical isolator switch market is categorized into three main types: Single Break Isolator, Double Break Isolator, and Pantograph Type Isolator. Single Break Isolators operate with one break in the circuit, making them compact and suitable for lower voltage applications. Double Break Isolators feature dual breaks for enhanced reliability and are preferred for medium to high voltage systems. Pantograph Type Isolators utilize a unique design for optimal space efficiency and are often used in overhead power lines, providing flexibility in power distribution.</p></p>
