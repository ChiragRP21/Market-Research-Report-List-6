<p><p>The Online COD Analyzers market comprises different methods for measuring chemical oxygen demand (COD) in water quality monitoring. The Potassium Dichromate Method, widely used due to its reliability, quantifies organic matter by oxidizing samples in acidic conditions. The Potassium Permanganate Method offers a faster, less hazardous alternative, utilizing a permanganate solution for oxidation, ideal for less complex samples. Other methods include various advanced techniques like ultraviolet and photometric approaches, catering to diverse industrial applications and specific environmental monitoring needs.</p></p>
