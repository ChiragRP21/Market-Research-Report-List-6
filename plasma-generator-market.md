AC Power Systems employ alternating current, enabling efficient plasma generation for larger-scale processes. RF Power Systems use radio frequency energy to generate plasma, allowing for precise control and high-frequency operation, making them ideal for applications like semiconductor manufacturing and surface treatment.</p></p>
