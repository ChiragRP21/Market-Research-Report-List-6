<p><p>The hearing screening apparatus market includes various technologies such as Otoacoustic Emissions (OAE) and Auditory Brainstem Response (ABR) systems, among others. OAE devices measure sound waves produced in the inner ear, helping to identify cochlear function, particularly in newborns. ABR systems assess the brain's auditory pathways by measuring responses to sound stimuli, useful for diagnosing hearing loss. Other devices in the market may include behavioral audiometry tools and selective audiometry tests, catering to diverse needs in hearing assessment and diagnosis.</p></p>
