<p><ul><li>AGM Battery</li><li>Gel Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SMF battery market primarily includes AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) and Gel batteries, both designed for maintenance-free performance. AGM batteries utilize a fiberglass mat to absorb electrolyte, offering superior vibration resistance and rapid discharge capabilities, making them ideal for high-demand applications. Gel batteries, on the other hand, use a silica gel electrolyte, providing excellent deep-cycle performance and reduced risk of leakage. Both types cater to sectors requiring reliability and safety, such as telecommunications, automotive, and renewable energy.</p></p>
