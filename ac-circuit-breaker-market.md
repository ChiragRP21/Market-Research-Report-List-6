<p><ul><li>Solid-state AC Circuit Breaker</li><li>Hybrid AC Circuit Breaker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AC Circuit Breaker market consists of two primary types: solid-state AC circuit breakers and hybrid AC circuit breakers. Solid-state AC circuit breakers utilize semiconductor devices to achieve rapid and precise switching, ensuring enhanced reliability and minimal wear. In contrast, hybrid AC circuit breakers combine conventional electromechanical components with solid-state technology, offering benefits like high-speed operation and improved protection while retaining the robustness of traditional systems. Both types are essential for modern electrical systems, addressing increasing power demands and ensuring safety.</p></p>
