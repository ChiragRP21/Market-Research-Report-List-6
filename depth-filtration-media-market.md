<p><p>The depth filtration media market comprises several key types, each serving distinct applications. Activated carbon is utilized for its adsorptive properties, effectively removing impurities and contaminants. Diatomaceous earth, made from fossilized algae, excels in fine particle filtration due to its porous structure. Cellulose-based media provide an eco-friendly option, suitable for various liquid and gas filtration. Perlite, a volcanic glass, offers lightweight filtration solutions with excellent thermal and sound insulation. Together, these media cater to diverse industrial and environmental filtration needs.</p></p>
