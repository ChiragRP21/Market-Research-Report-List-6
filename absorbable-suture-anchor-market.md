<p><p>An absorbable suture anchor is a biomedical device used in surgical procedures to secure soft tissues to bone. These anchors are made of biodegradable materials that eventually dissolve in the body, eliminating the need for a subsequent removal procedure. Their primary applications are in orthopedics, sports medicine, and reconstructive surgeries, allowing for effective tissue fixation while minimizing long-term complications associated with permanent implants.</p><p>The absorbable suture anchor market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the rising incidence of orthopedic injuries and the increasing popularity of minimally invasive surgical techniques. Furthermore, advancements in biomaterials and manufacturing processes enhance the effectiveness and safety of these anchors, further fueling market demand. The shift towards outpatient and same-day discharge procedures is also boosting the utilization of absorbable suture anchors due to their reduced risk of postoperative complications.</p><p>The Absorbable Suture Anchor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.00% during the forecast period.
