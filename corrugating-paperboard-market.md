<p><p>The corrugating paperboard market consists of several types, primarily categorized by the number of walls. Single wall paperboard features one layer of fluting between linerboards, offering moderate strength for lightweight packaging. Double wall paperboard has two layers of fluting, providing enhanced durability and resistance for heavier loads. Triple wall paperboard, with three layers of fluting, is designed for maximum strength and protection in heavy-duty applications. Other types may include specialized configurations for unique packaging needs, enhancing versatility in the market.</p></p>
