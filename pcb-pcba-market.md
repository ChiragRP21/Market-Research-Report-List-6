<p><p>The PCB and PCBA market includes various types: </p><p>1. **Rigid 1-2 Sided**: Basic boards with one or two conductive layers.</p><p>2. **Standard Multilayer**: Boards with multiple layers for complex circuitry.</p><p>3. **HDI (High-Density Interconnect)**: Features finer lines and smaller pads for compact designs.</p><p>4. **Microvia or Build-Up**: Uses tiny holes for connections, enabling high-density layouts.</p><p>5. **IC Substrate**: Supports integrated circuits, often with high thermal performance.</p><p>6. **Flexible Circuits**: Bendable boards for dynamic applications.</p><p>7. **Rigid Flex**: Combines rigid and flexible technologies for versatility.</p><p>8. **Others**: Encompasses specialty types like printed electronics or specialty materials.</p></p>
