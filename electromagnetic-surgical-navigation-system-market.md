<p><strong>The Electromagnetic Surgical Navigation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hybrid Navigation</li><li>Electromagnetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electromagnetic Surgical Navigation System market can be categorized into two main types: hybrid navigation and electromagnetic navigation. Hybrid navigation combines optical and electromagnetic technologies, enhancing accuracy and flexibility in surgical procedures by allowing real-time tracking of instruments in relation to the patient’s anatomy. Electromagnetic navigation, on the other hand, uses magnetic fields to determine the position of surgical tools without line-of-sight limitations, facilitating minimally invasive surgeries and improving workflow efficiency in operating rooms. Both types contribute significantly to precision in surgical interventions.</p></p>
