<p><p>Automotive gesture recognition technology comprises two main market types: touchless systems and touch-based systems. Touchless systems utilize sensors to interpret driver and passenger gestures without physical contact, enhancing safety and convenience by minimizing distractions. In contrast, touch-based systems require users to physically interact with surfaces, such as touchscreens or buttons, to execute commands. Both technologies aim to improve user experience and vehicle interface, facilitating intuitive control over various functions while maintaining focus on the road.</p></p>
