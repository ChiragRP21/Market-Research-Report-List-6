<p><p>Organic CMOS image sensors are categorized into two main types - linear image sensors and area image sensors. Linear image sensors capture images line by line, making them suitable for applications like barcode scanning and document scanning. Area image sensors capture the entire image at once, making them ideal for applications like digital cameras and smartphones. Both types of sensors offer high sensitivity, low noise, and high resolution, making them a popular choice for various imaging applications.</p></p>
