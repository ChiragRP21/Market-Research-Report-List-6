<p><p>The gas flow measuring instruments market comprises various types, including orifice, vortex, and turbine measuring instruments. Orifice measuring instruments utilize a flow-restricting orifice to determine flow rates based on pressure differences. Vortex measuring instruments detect vortices created by fluid flow, translating them into flow measurements. Turbine measuring instruments operate by using a rotating turbine that measures gas flow based on the speed of rotation. Each type offers unique advantages and applications, catering to different industrial needs and accuracy requirements.</p></p>
