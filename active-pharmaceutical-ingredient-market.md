<p><ul><li>Synthetic API</li><li>Biotech API</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Active Pharmaceutical Ingredient (API) market comprises mainly two types: Synthetic APIs and Biotech APIs. Synthetic APIs are chemically manufactured using various chemical processes and are often utilized for traditional drugs due to their cost-effectiveness and scalability. In contrast, Biotech APIs are derived from living organisms through biotechnological methods, such as fermentation and cell culture, primarily used in advanced therapies like monoclonal antibodies and vaccines. Both sectors are vital for producing effective pharmaceuticals, catering to diverse medical needs.</p></p>
