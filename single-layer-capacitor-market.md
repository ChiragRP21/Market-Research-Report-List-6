<p><ul><li>Surface Mount</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single layer capacitors are available in two main market types: surface mount and through hole. Surface mount capacitors are smaller in size and can be directly soldered onto the surface of a printed circuit board, making them ideal for compact electronic devices. On the other hand, through hole capacitors have leads that are inserted through holes on the PCB and soldered on the other side, offering a more secure connection. Both types have their advantages and are widely used in various electronic applications.</p></p>
