<p><strong>The Automatic Titration System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulometric Titration</li><li>Volumetric Titration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Titration System market comprises two main types: coulometric titration and volumetric titration. Coulometric titration involves the generation of titrant through an electrochemical reaction, allowing for precise measurement of small quantities, ideal for trace analysis in various industries. In contrast, volumetric titration relies on the direct addition of a titrant of known concentration to a sample until a reaction endpoint is reached, making it suitable for larger sample volumes and diverse applications in laboratories and quality control environments.</p></p>
