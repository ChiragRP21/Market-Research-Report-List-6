<p><p>The adhesive for electronics market is divided into two main types: non-electrically conductive and electrically conductive adhesives. Non-electrically conductive adhesives are primarily used for bonding components where electrical isolation is essential, such as in insulation applications. These adhesives are crucial for ensuring the safety and performance of electronic devices. Conversely, electrically conductive adhesives facilitate electrical connections between components, often replacing traditional soldering methods. They are vital for enhancing performance and reliability in various electronic assemblies, including circuit boards and sensors.</p></p>
