<p><ul><li>Hot Mix</li><li>Cold Mix</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ready Mixed Asphalt market primarily includes two types: Hot Mix Asphalt and Cold Mix Asphalt. Hot Mix Asphalt involves heating aggregate and asphalt binder for optimal workability and stronger performance, making it ideal for high-traffic roads. Cold Mix Asphalt, on the other hand, uses emulsified asphalt for easier application in cooler temperatures, suitable for patching and maintenance work. Both types serve distinct purposes within the road construction and maintenance sectors, ensuring durability and efficiency in various climatic conditions.</p></p>
