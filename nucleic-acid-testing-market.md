<p><ul><li>Transcription-mediated Amplification (TMA)</li><li>Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR)</li><li>Ligase Chain Reaction (LCR)</li><li>Whole Genome Sequencing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Nucleic Acid Testing (NAT) market encompasses various techniques for detecting genetic material. Transcription-mediated Amplification (TMA) amplifies RNA, allowing for sensitive pathogen detection. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) is widely used for DNA amplification, essential in diagnostics and research. Ligase Chain Reaction (LCR) focuses on amplifying specific DNA sequences using ligation-based techniques, enhancing accuracy. Whole Genome Sequencing provides comprehensive insights into an organism's complete genetic makeup, enabling advanced research and personalized medicine applications in the healthcare sector.</p></p>
