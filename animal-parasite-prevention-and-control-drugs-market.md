<p><p>The animal parasite prevention and control drugs market encompasses three primary types: topical, injection, and oral medications. Injection medications involve administering drugs via a syringe, offering systemic protection against various internal and external parasites. Oral medications are ingested by the animal, effectively targeting internal parasites such as worms. Each type caters to specific needs, ensuring effective parasite management in veterinary care.</p></p>
