<p><p>The FR-4 PCB market comprises three main types: single-sided, double-sided, and multilayer PCBs. Single-sided FR-4 PCBs have components mounted on one side, making them cost-effective for simple applications. Double-sided FR-4 PCBs feature components on both sides, allowing for greater design complexity and functionality. Multilayer FR-4 PCBs consist of multiple layers of circuitry, enabling high-density designs and advanced electronic functions, often used in intricate devices like smartphones and computers. Each type caters to different performance and complexity requirements.</p></p>
