<p><p>Fish protein powder (FPP) is derived from fish and offers a rich source of high-quality protein. The market primarily comprises fish protein concentrate (FPC) and fish protein isolate (FPI). FPC retains more of the natural fats and nutrients, making it suitable for various food applications. In contrast, FPI undergoes extensive processing to remove fats, providing a purer protein source often used in supplements and functional foods. Both types cater to health-conscious consumers seeking alternative protein sources.</p></p>
