<p><p>The car Bluetooth microphones market features two primary types: ECM (Electret Condenser Microphones) and MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) microphones. ECM microphones are known for their excellent audio quality and sensitivity, making them suitable for hands-free calling and in-car communication. In contrast, MEMS microphones are smaller, more durable, and offer superior sound processing capabilities, often providing enhanced noise cancellation. Both types cater to the growing demand for voice-activated technologies in vehicles, enhancing user experience and safety.</p></p>
