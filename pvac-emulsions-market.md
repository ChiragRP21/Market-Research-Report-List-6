<p><p>PVAc Emulsions are available in two main types based on their viscosity: High Viscosity and Low Viscosity. High Viscosity PVAc emulsions are thicker in consistency and are commonly used in applications requiring stronger adhesion properties such as woodworking and construction. On the other hand, Low Viscosity PVAc emulsions have a thinner consistency and are more commonly used in applications where a smooth finish and easy spreadability are desired, such as in paint and coatings. Both types offer unique properties catering to specific application requirements.</p></p>
