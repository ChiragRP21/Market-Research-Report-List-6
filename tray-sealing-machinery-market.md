Semi-automatic machines streamline the sealing process with some automated features, suitable for moderate production levels. Fully automatic machines operate independently, handling sealing with minimal human intervention, making them perfect for high-volume and efficiency-driven environments. Each type caters to different production needs, budgets, and operational scales.</p></p>
