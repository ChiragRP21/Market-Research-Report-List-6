<p><p>The Phase Shift Masks (PSM) market encompasses various types that enhance photolithography for semiconductor manufacturing. Halftone Masks utilize attenuated features to improve resolution. Levenson Masks employ an alternating phase approach for better edge definition. RimPSM focuses on enhancing the rim area to reduce diffraction. Sub-Resolution PSM includes patterns below resolution limits to improve process performance. Self-alignment PSM aids in alignment without extra steps. Chrome-less PSM utilizes entirely transparent materials, and Complex PSM integrates multiple strategies for advanced patterning capabilities.</p></p>
