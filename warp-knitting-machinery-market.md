<p><p>The warp knitting machinery market can be divided into two main types: Raschel and Tricot warp knitting machines. Raschel warp knitting machines are known for producing heavier, more complex fabrics, often used in applications like lace, netting, and technical textiles, thanks to their ability to create elaborate patterns and structures. In contrast, Tricot warp knitting machines are designed for lighter fabrics, often used in lingerie, sportswear, and tulle, focusing on smooth finishes and versatility for various textile applications.</p></p>
