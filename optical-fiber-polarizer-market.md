<p><p>The optical fiber polarizer market features three main types: PM-PM (Polarization Maintaining-Polarization Maintaining) fibers, which maintain the polarization state of light; SM-PM (Single Mode-Polarization Maintaining) fibers, designed for single-mode applications with polarization control; and M-SM (Multimode-Single Mode) fibers, which balance multimode and single-mode benefits. Each type serves specific applications in telecommunications, sensors, and lasers, offering unique advantages in polarization preservation, signal integrity, and performance in various environments and configurations.</p></p>
