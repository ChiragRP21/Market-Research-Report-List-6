<p><strong>The Froth Flotation Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cell-to-Cell Flotation</li><li>Free-Flow Flotation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The froth flotation device market comprises two primary types: cell-to-cell flotation and free-flow flotation. Cell-to-cell flotation involves a series of cells that transport slurries sequentially, maximizing mineral recovery through controlled airflow and bubble formation. In contrast, free-flow flotation features a continuous flow of slurry, allowing for simultaneous collection and separation of minerals, which enhances efficiency. Both types are essential for optimizing mineral extraction processes in various industries, including mining and recycling.</p></p>
