<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Diet Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Soluble Diet Fiber</li><li>Insoluble Diet Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dietary fiber market is primarily divided into two types: soluble and insoluble dietary fiber. Soluble dietary fiber dissolves in water, forming a gel-like substance that can help regulate blood sugar levels and lower cholesterol. It's often found in oats, fruits, and legumes. Insoluble dietary fiber, on the other hand, does not dissolve and aids in promoting digestive health by adding bulk to stool. It's commonly found in whole grains, nuts, and vegetables, supporting regular bowel movements and overall gut health.</p></p>
