<p><p>The Liquid Crystal Tunable Filter market is divided into two primary types: Visible (VIS) and Near-Infrared (NIR). VIS filters are designed to manipulate light within the visible spectrum, enabling applications in imaging, spectroscopy, and optical communication. NIR filters cater to wavelengths beyond visible light, focusing on applications such as environmental monitoring, biomedical imaging, and industrial sensing. Both types leverage liquid crystal technology for precise light modulation, enhancing their utility across various scientific and commercial sectors.</p></p>
