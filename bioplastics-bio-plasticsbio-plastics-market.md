<p><p>Bioplastics are sustainable alternatives to traditional plastics, derived from renewable biological sources. The market comprises several types: Bio-PET is derived from plant-based materials, offering similar properties to traditional PET. Bio-PE is made from renewable resources, maintaining the characteristics of conventional polyethylene. Starch blends combine starch with plastics for enhanced biodegradability. PLA (polylactic acid) is sourced from corn starch, known for its compostability. PHA (polyhydroxyalkanoates) is produced by microbial fermentation, offering versatility. Other bioplastics include various innovative formulations and composites.</p></p>
