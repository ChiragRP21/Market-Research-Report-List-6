<p><p>Light Guide Plates (LGPs) are essential components in Liquid Crystal Television (LCTV) displays, enhancing uniform light distribution. The market for LGPs is categorized into Print LGPs and Print-less LGPs. Print LGPs involve a printed design on the surface, utilizing micro-patterns to control light diffusion and improve brightness. In contrast, Print-less LGPs use advanced manufacturing techniques to achieve similar effects without the need for printed patterns, offering greater production efficiency and cost-effectiveness while maintaining optical performance. Both types play a crucial role in optimizing display quality.</p></p>
