Low voltage circuit breakers operate up to 1,000 volts and are commonly used in residential and small commercial applications for overload and short-circuit protection. Medium voltage circuit breakers function between 1 kV and 36 kV, serving industrial and utility sectors. High voltage circuit breakers operate above 36 kV, crucial for managing electrical grids and substations, providing protection in high-demand environments.</p></p>
