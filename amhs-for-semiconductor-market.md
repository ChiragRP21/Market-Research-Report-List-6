<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The AMHS for Semiconductor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>STK</li><li>OHT</li><li>OHS</li><li>RGV</li><li>AGV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated Material Handling Systems (AMHS) in the semiconductor market include various types designed to optimize the transportation and storage of semiconductor wafers. </p><p>- **STK (Storage & Transport Kit)** systems manage inventory by storing and transporting wafers efficiently. </p><p>- **OHT (Overhead Hoist Transport)** systems utilize overhead tracks to move materials, maximizing floor space. </p><p>- **OHS (Overhead System)** refers to systems that transport materials above the production line. </p><p>- **RGV (Rail Guided Vehicle)** systems move goods along fixed paths, while </p><p>- **AGV (Automated Guided Vehicle)** systems are mobile vehicles that navigate independently. Each type enhances productivity and reduces human error in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
