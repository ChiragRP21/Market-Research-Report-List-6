<p><p>The railway vehicle coupler market comprises three main types: automatic couplers, semi-automatic couplers, and semi-permanent couplers. Automatic couplers facilitate quick and efficient train assembly and disassembly without manual intervention, enhancing operational speed. Semi-automatic couplers require some manual assistance for connection but provide a blend of efficiency and flexibility. Semi-permanent couplers are designed for longer-term connections, often used in specific applications like freight trains, ensuring secure linkage and stability over extended periods while limiting separation.</p></p>
