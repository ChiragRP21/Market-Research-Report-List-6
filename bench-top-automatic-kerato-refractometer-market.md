Spherical Power refers to the measurement of the overall curvature of the cornea, essential for determining the spherical lens prescription. In contrast, Cylindrical Power focuses on measuring the astigmatism components, which arise from variations in corneal curvature. Both types are crucial for accurate vision correction assessments, influencing the design of corrective lenses for patients with different vision needs.</p></p>
