<p><p>The Medical Hand Orthosis (HO) market includes two primary types: dynamic orthotics and static orthotics. Dynamic orthotics are designed to facilitate movement and support active rehabilitation, providing controlled motion while stabilizing the hand and fingers. In contrast, static orthotics offer rigid support to immobilize the hand, promoting rest and healing for injuries or conditions. Both types cater to different therapeutic needs, improving functionality and patient outcomes in managing hand-related injuries and disorders.</p></p>
