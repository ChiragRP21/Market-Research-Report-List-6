<p><p>The fast thyristor market comprises two primary types: capsule type and stud type. Capsule type thyristors are compact, typically used in applications requiring high switching speeds while maintaining a small footprint. They are ideal for circuit protection and power control. In contrast, stud type thyristors are designed for high power applications, featuring a larger size and robust construction that allows for efficient heat dissipation and improved reliability in demanding environments, making them suitable for industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
