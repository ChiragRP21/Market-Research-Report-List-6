<p><p>The Digital Fabrication Inkjet Inks market comprises two primary types: dye-based and pigment-based inks. Dye-based inks are known for their vibrant colors and are often used in applications requiring exceptional image quality on various substrates. They dissolve in liquid and typically offer a broader color gamut. In contrast, pigment-based inks consist of solid particles suspended in a liquid, providing superior UV stability and resistance to fading. This makes them ideal for outdoor applications and long-lasting prints.</p></p>
