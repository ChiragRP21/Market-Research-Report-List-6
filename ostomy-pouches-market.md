<p><p>The ostomy pouch market consists primarily of two types: one-piece and two-piece bags. One-piece bags combine the skin barrier and pouch into a single unit, offering ease of use and convenience, making them popular for short-term use or active lifestyles. In contrast, two-piece bags feature a separate skin barrier and pouch, allowing for easier pouch changes without removing the barrier, preferred for long-term use. Both types cater to diverse patient needs and preferences, influencing market demand and trends.</p></p>
