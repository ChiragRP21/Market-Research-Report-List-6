<p><p>In the automobile differential market, various types cater to different performance needs. An anti-slip differential enhances traction by distributing power to the wheels with the most grip, minimizing wheel spin. The double worm differential utilizes two worm gears to improve torque distribution and efficiency, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Other market variations include limited-slip differentials that balance power between wheels during turns and electronic differentials that optimize performance dynamically. Each type plays a crucial role in vehicle handling and control.</p></p>
