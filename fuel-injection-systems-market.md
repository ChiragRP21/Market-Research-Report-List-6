<p><p>The fuel injection systems market is primarily divided into two types: Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI) and Mechanical Fuel Injection (MFI). EFI utilizes electronic sensors and computer controls to optimize fuel delivery for improved efficiency, performance, and emissions. In contrast, MFI relies on mechanical components, such as pumps and injectors, to deliver fuel based on engine demands, offering simplicity and reliability. The choice between these systems often depends on application requirements, cost considerations, and performance goals in automotive and industrial sectors.</p></p>
