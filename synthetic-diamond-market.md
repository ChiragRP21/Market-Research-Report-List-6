<p><p>The synthetic diamond market consists of various grain types defined by their manufacturing processes and applications. RVD (Real-Volume Diamond) grain is primarily used for cutting and grinding tools. MBD (Microcrystalline Diamond) grain features smaller crystal sizes, enhancing precision in polishing applications. SCD (Single-Crystal Diamond) grain offers superior hardness for industrial applications. SMD (Synthetic Mono-Crystalline Diamond) grain provides high-quality performance in advanced machining. DMD (Diamond Micro Powder) grain is utilized for fine grinding and as an abrasive, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
