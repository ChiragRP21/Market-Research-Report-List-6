<p><ul><li>Vibration Analysis and Diagnostics</li><li>Ultrasound Testing (Material Thickness/Flaw Testing)</li><li>Acoustic Emission (Airborne Ultrasound)</li><li>Infrared Thermography</li><li>Motor Condition Monitoring and Motor Current Signature Analysis (MCSA)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Condition monitoring systems encompass various market types essential for maintaining equipment efficiency and safety. Vibration analysis detects mechanical issues in machinery. Ultrasound testing assesses material integrity through thickness and flaw detection. Acoustic emission monitors sound waves during stress to identify leaks or fractures. Infrared thermography analyzes thermal patterns to spot overheating components. Motor condition monitoring evaluates performance and health, while Motor Current Signature Analysis (MCSA) examines electrical signals for anomalies. Other methods include oil analysis and visual inspections, enhancing overall asset management.</p></p>
