<p><strong>The LED Drive Power Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Power Supply</li><li>Built-In Power Supply</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED drive power market is primarily segmented into two types: external power supply and built-in power supply. External power supplies are standalone units that provide necessary voltage and current to LED systems, offering flexibility in design and ease of replacement. In contrast, built-in power supplies are integrated into the LED fixture itself, leading to more compact designs and typically improved efficiency. Both types cater to various applications, influencing performance, installation, and maintenance of LED lighting solutions.</p></p>
