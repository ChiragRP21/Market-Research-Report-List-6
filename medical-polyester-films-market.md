<p><p>The medical polyester films market is divided into three types based on thickness: less than 100μm, 100-300μm, and more than 300μm. Films under 100μm are typically used for applications requiring high flexibility and breathability, while those in the 100-300μm range offer a balance of strength and flexibility, suitable for packaging and protective barriers. Films exceeding 300μm provide enhanced durability and are often used in more robust applications, such as surgical drapes and sterilization pouches.</p></p>
