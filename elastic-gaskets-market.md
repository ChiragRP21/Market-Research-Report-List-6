<p><p>The elastic gaskets market features two primary types: saddle gaskets and waveform gaskets. Saddle gaskets are designed with a rounded profile to ensure a tight seal against flanges, providing durability and reliability in various applications. Waveform gaskets, on the other hand, utilize a wavy surface pattern, allowing for flexibility and adaptability to uneven surfaces, enhancing their sealing capability. Both types cater to different demands in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, ensuring optimal performance in sealing solutions.</p></p>
