<p><p>Wireless System-on-Chip (SoC) market comprises three main types: 802.15.4, WiFi, and Bluetooth. 802.15.4 is commonly used in low-power, low-data-rate applications like home automation and industrial control. WiFi is widely used for high-speed internet access in homes, offices, and public spaces. Bluetooth is popular for short-range communication between devices like smartphones, headphones, and speakers. Each type caters to specific needs and requirements in the wireless communication industry, providing different levels of data transfer speeds, power consumption, and range.</p></p>
